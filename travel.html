Mexico 2018 and 2019

In June 2018 and again in June 2019, I went to Ensenada, BC, Mexico. We were there for 11 days per trip where we handed out food boxes, gave out flyers for the church, did manual labor projects, and played with children. It is one of my favorite trips I have ever been on and I learn more about myself, my friends, and God than I do anywhere else.


Europe 2019

In Spring 2019, I spent 14 days in Europe. We flew from Memphis, TN to Atlanta, GA, Atlanta, GA to New York, NY, and from New York, NY to Dublin, IRL. After 2 days in Dublin we drove up to belfast where we stayed the night. From there we hopped on a ferry to Cairnryan, SCT. The remainder of our trip was in Edinburgh, Sterling, and Edinburgh again.
